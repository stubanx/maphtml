Temple Name:
Shri Krishna Balram Temple, Vrindavan (ISCKON)

The Krishna Balaram Temple in Vrindavan, India, is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and his brother Balarama. It was established by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, the founder of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), in the 1970s. This temple is known for its beautiful architecture, vibrant devotional atmosphere, and numerous spiritual activities. It attracts a large number of devotees and tourists seeking to experience the rich tradition of Bhakti (devotional worship) associated with Lord Krishna.
