Temple Name:
Old Madan Mohan ji Temple, Vrindavan

The Madan Mohan Temple, located in Vrindavan, India, is dedicated to Lord Krishna, who is worshipped in the form of Madan Mohan. This temple is one of the oldest in Vrindavan, believed to have been established in the 16th century by the renowned saint Sanatan Goswami. The temple is a popular pilgrimage site and features intricate architecture and beautiful deities, attracting many devotees throughout the year.
