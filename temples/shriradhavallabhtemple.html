Temple Name:
Shri Radha Vallabh Ji Temple, Vrindavan

Radhavallabh Mandir, located in Vrindavan, India, is a prominent temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and his consort Radha. It was established in the 16th century by the revered saint Vallabhacharya and represents the Pushti Marg sect of Vaishnavism. The temple is known for its exquisite architecture, beautiful idols, and vibrant religious festivals that attract numerous devotees.
